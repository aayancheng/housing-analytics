Feature 01
Full GTA Submarket Breakdown
All major GTA submarkets on one comparative chart — 416 (Toronto proper) vs 905 regions (Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Ajax, Whitby). See where the premium concentrates and how the gap has evolved since 2005.
Feature 02
Affordability Index
Percentage of median household income required to service the mortgage on a median detached home — tracked annually from 1995 to 2025. The defining story of Toronto housing: incomes grew 60%, prices grew 400%.
Feature 03
Policy Event Overlay
Every major policy intervention annotated directly on the 30-year chart: OSFI stress test B-20 (2018), Ontario Non-Resident Speculation Tax (2017), federal foreign buyer ban (2023), First Home Savings Account. See what moved the market and what didn't.
Feature 04
Price-to-Rent Yield by Property Type
Detached vs semi-detached vs condo — gross rental yield and price-to-rent ratio tracked over time. The condo collapse of 2023–24 (77% of investors cash-flow negative) vs the relative resilience of detached. Where does fundamental value lie?
